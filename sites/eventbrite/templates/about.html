About Eventbrite

Eventbrite is the global marketplace for live experiences. We help creators sell tickets, manage attendees, and grow their audience — for events ranging from a 10-person yoga workshop to a 100,000-person festival.

Our mission

To bring the world together through live experiences.

By the numbers

  • 5M+ creators trust Eventbrite each year
  • 290M+ tickets distributed since 2006
  • 180+ countries served
  • 3,500+ event categories supported

Leadership

Founded in 2006 by Kevin Hartz, Julia Hartz, and Renaud Visage in San Francisco.
