What's covered

  • Personal property (theft, fire, water damage)
  • Personal liability ($100,000 default)
  • Medical payments to guests ($1,000 default)
  • Loss-of-use (hotel + meals during repairs)
  • Identity-fraud restoration

What's not covered

  • Floods (need a separate flood policy)
  • Earthquakes (separate rider)
  • Damage you cause to the structure (landlord's policy)

FAQ

Is renters insurance required? Most modern leases require $100,000 liability minimum. Check your lease.

How fast can I get a policy? Most quotes can be bound the same day.

Can my landlord see my policy? Yes — you can add them as an "interested party" so they receive notice if it lapses.
