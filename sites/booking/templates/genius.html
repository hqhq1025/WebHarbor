Frequently asked Genius questions

Is Genius really free?

Yes. Create a free Booking.com account and you're automatically Level 1. There are no membership fees, ever.

Do my Genius rewards expire?

Your discounts do not expire as long as your account stays active. Level progression is based on bookings completed over a rolling 2-year period.

Which properties accept Genius?

Look for the blue Genius badge on property listings. Over 390,000 properties participate worldwide.

Can I use Genius on any room type?

Genius discounts apply to selected room types at participating properties. Eligibility is clearly marked at checkout.
