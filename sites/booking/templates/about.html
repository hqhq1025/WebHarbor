About Booking.com

Our story

Founded in 1996 in Amsterdam, Booking.com has grown from a small Dutch start-up to one of the world's leading digital travel companies. Part of Booking Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: BKNG), we connect travellers with the widest choice of incredible places to stay.

Our mission

We aim to make it easier for everyone to experience the world. Whether it's a quick business trip or a longer family holiday, we have just what you need.

The numbers

With over 28 million accommodation listings across 220+ countries and territories, travellers can find something for every budget and taste.
