Cancellation questions

How do I know whether my booking has been cancelled?

When a booking is cancelled, you'll receive a cancellation confirmation email at the address on the reservation, and the booking status in My bookings will change to "Cancelled". If you don't see the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder and then open your account's bookings page — the status shown there is authoritative.

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to My bookings in your account dashboard, open the reservation you want to cancel, and click Cancel booking. Most reservations with "Free cancellation" can be cancelled online in one click; others may ask you to contact the property directly.

Will I get a refund when I cancel?

That depends on the cancellation policy shown at the time of booking. If your reservation included Free cancellation and you cancel before the cut-off date, you'll receive a full refund to the card used for payment within 5-10 business days.

Can the property cancel my booking?

Only in rare cases (e.g. overbooking or emergency closure). If that happens, Booking will email you immediately and help you find a comparable alternative at the same or better price.

Why was my booking cancelled automatically?

Automatic cancellations usually happen when payment could not be completed by the property's confirmation deadline. Check the card you used, update your payment details in your account, and rebook if needed.

Can I cancel a non-refundable booking?

You can always cancel it, but the property has the right to charge the full amount. Some properties offer a partial goodwill refund — it's worth reaching out via the booking's messaging tab.

Managing your bookings

Can I modify my dates?

Yes — go to My bookings and select Modify dates on eligible reservations.

How do I add a guest or change room type?

Open the reservation, click Manage booking, and choose the option you need. If the change isn't available online, message the property directly from that page.

Payment & charges

Is my card charged immediately?

This depends on the property. Some charge at booking, others at check-in. See the booking details for when your card will be billed.

Which currencies are supported?

Prices are stored in USD and converted at checkout. Use the currency selector at the top of the page to view prices in USD, EUR, GBP, CNY or JPY.

Genius programme

How do I unlock Genius discounts?

Complete two stays within two years to reach Genius Level 1 and start seeing 10% discounts on participating properties. See the Genius page for full details.

Staying with us

What are standard check-in / check-out times?

Most properties check in from 3 PM and check out by 11 AM. Specific times are listed on each property page under House rules.
