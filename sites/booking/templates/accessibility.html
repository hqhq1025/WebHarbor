Accessibility statement

Booking.com is committed to making its digital products usable by everyone, regardless of ability. This site targets WCAG 2.1 Level AA conformance.

Conformance status

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at conformance level AA. Most of the site has been audited against this standard, and our last third-party audit was completed on 2026-04-18.

What this means in practice

  • All informational images carry text alternatives (alt text).
  • Colour-contrast ratios meet WCAG-AA thresholds (4.5:1 for body copy, 3:1 for large text).
  • Every interactive control is reachable and operable via keyboard.
  • Forms include explicit labels and accessible validation messages.
  • Pages declare a single primary <h1> and use semantic heading hierarchy.
  • ARIA landmarks (banner, navigation, main, contentinfo) wrap structural regions.

Accessible-room features we support

Booking.com surfaces the following accessibility features on property pages whenever they are confirmed by the host:

  1. Wheelchair-accessible entrance
  2. Accessible parking spaces near entrance
  3. Step-free ground-floor access throughout the public areas
  4. Lift / elevator
  5. Roll-in shower
  6. Grab rails in bathrooms and toilets
  7. Lowered counters at reception
  8. Visual / vibrating fire alarms
  9. Hearing-impaired guest equipment
  10. Braille signage and lift buttons
  11. Service dogs welcome
  12. Visual aids in lifts and stairwells

Limitations and feedback

Some third-party content (embedded maps, AMP cross-link badges) may not yet meet full AA conformance. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please contact accessibility@booking.example.
