Data categories

schedule Timeline (Location History)

A private map of places you've visited, recorded only when you have Location History turned on.

Go to your timeline →
bookmark Saved places & lists

Places you've saved to Want to go, Favorites, Starred, and any custom lists you've made.

View your saved places →
star_outline Your reviews

Reviews and ratings you've contributed for places you've visited.

photo_library Photos

Photos you've uploaded for places. Public photos can be viewed by anyone; you can delete them at any time.

luggage Trips

Trips you've planned with stops and notes. Trips you've completed are kept for your reference.

View your trips →
search Maps search activity

Recent searches in Maps appear as suggestions when you start typing. You can clear search history any time.

Privacy controls

  • Pause Location History — stop recording places you visit at any time.
  • Auto-delete — choose to have Maps data older than 3, 18, or 36 months auto-removed.
  • Incognito mode in Maps — browse and navigate without saving activity to your account.
  • Delete your data — remove individual items or all data from a category.
