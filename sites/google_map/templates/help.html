Help & support

Answers to common questions about using Google Maps.

Getting started

How do I search for a place?

Use the search bar at the top of the left panel. Type any place name, category, or city and press Enter.

How do I save a place?

Open any place's detail page and click Save. You can organize saved places into custom lists.

How do I get directions?

Click the directions icon in the search bar or visit the Directions page from the left rail. Enter your starting point and destination.

Trips & lists

What are trips?

Trips are multi-stop itineraries. You can create a trip for a vacation, add places as stops, and mark the trip as completed when you're done.

Can I share my lists?

In this educational version, lists are private to your account. You can still organize places by theme and color.

How do I delete a list?

Open the list detail page and click Delete. Note: default lists like "Want to go" and "Favorites" cannot be deleted.

Account

How do I change my password?

Go to your Account page and click "Change password". You'll need to enter your current password.

How do I delete my account?

Visit Account → Danger zone. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your saved places, trips, and reviews.
