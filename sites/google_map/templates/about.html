About Google Maps

Find your way across {{ 38 }}+ cities, discover hundreds of real places, and plan unforgettable trips.

What is this?

Google Maps helps you navigate your world: faster, easier, and more reliably. Browse restaurants, hotels, attractions, museums, parks, and shops. Save your favorite spots into custom lists, plan multi-stop trips, and keep a personal timeline of everywhere you've been.

Features

  • Explore — browse thousands of curated places across {{ 8 }} categories.
  • Save — organize places into custom lists like "Want to go" or "Favorites".
  • Plan trips — build multi-stop itineraries with notes and dates.
  • Directions — estimate distance and travel time between any two places.
  • Reviews — read what others think and share your own opinions.
  • Timeline — check in at places you visit and see your travel history.

Our mission

We aim to organize the world's places and make them universally accessible and useful. From the Eiffel Tower to your neighborhood coffee shop, Maps helps you find what matters.
