Contribute to Google Maps

Help others by sharing what you know about the places around you

Your contributions make Google Maps more useful for everyone. Whether you're sharing a photo of a great meal, writing a review of your favorite spot, or adding a missing place, every contribution helps travelers and locals alike.

Ways to contribute

star_outline Write reviews

Rate a place from 1 to 5 stars and share your experience. Your honest review helps others decide where to go.

add_a_photo Add photos

Upload photos of food, interiors, exteriors, and views. Photos give places a richer visual story.

edit_location_alt Edit place details

Suggest corrections to hours, phone numbers, addresses, or descriptions when something's out of date.

add_location_alt Add missing places

If a place isn't on the map, you can add it. Include name, address, category, and hours.

question_answer Answer questions

Help travelers by answering questions about places you know — wheelchair access, vegetarian options, kid-friendliness.

Local Guides program

Earn points and unlock perks as you contribute

Reviews, photos, answers, and edits earn points toward Local Guide levels. Higher levels unlock features like early access to new Google products and occasional Google Drive storage upgrades.

  • Level 1: 0 points
  • Level 4: 250 points + 1-month Google One trial
  • Level 6: 1,500 points + early access to new features
  • Level 10: 100,000 points + invitation to the annual Local Guides Summit
