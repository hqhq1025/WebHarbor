Database indexes

The flight result query filters by (origin, destination, departure date) and (airline, date). Composite indexes ix_flight_origin_dest_date and ix_flight_airline_date turn each query into an index seek instead of a table scan, which is what keeps LCP under 2 s even with 1.5M flight rows in the catalogue.
