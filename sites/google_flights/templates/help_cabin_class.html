Choosing a cabin class

  • Short-haul (under 3 hours): Premium Economy and Business are usually only available on wide-body equipment — most short-haul flights only sell Economy.
  • Long-haul overnight: Premium Economy is typically 30-50% more than Economy; Business 2-3x; First 4-6x. Lie-flat seats start at Business on most carriers.
  • Award redemption: First class points cost is usually only ~30% higher than Business for the same route — if you have the miles, the upgrade is usually the best value.
