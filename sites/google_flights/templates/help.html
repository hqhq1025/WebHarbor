Searching flights

Enter an origin and destination (city name, country, or IATA code), pick your dates, then click Search. Results can be sorted by Best, Cheapest, or Fastest, and filtered by stops, airline, cabin class, and price.

For multi-airport cities like New York or Tokyo, searching the city name returns flights from all airports in that city. Search a specific IATA code (JFK, NRT) to narrow down to one airport.

Booking and checkout

Add a flight to your bag from any flight detail page or search result, then go to Checkout. You can use a saved payment method or enter a new card. After confirming, you'll receive a PNR you can use to manage your booking from Trips.

Cancelling a booking

Open the booking from Trips and click Cancel. Cancellations are processed immediately; refund timing depends on the fare rules of the airline that operates the flight.

Tracking prices

On a flight detail page, click Track price to follow that itinerary. Your tracked flights are listed under Tracked.

Price alerts

Set a target price for any route from the Price alerts tab. We'll notify you when fares drop below your threshold.

Account and payment

Edit your profile, change your password, or manage saved payment methods from My account.
