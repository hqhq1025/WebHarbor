Voice assistant integration

Search flights, manage trips, and set price alerts hands-free. Google Flights exposes a stable voice intent surface that smart assistants (Alexa, Google Assistant, Siri) can dispatch to.

Supported assistants

  • Alexa — say "Alexa, ask Google Flights to find a flight from New York to London tomorrow."
  • Google Assistant — say "Hey Google, find me cheap flights to Tokyo next month."
  • Siri — say "Hey Siri, what's the cheapest flight from Boston to Paris this weekend?"
