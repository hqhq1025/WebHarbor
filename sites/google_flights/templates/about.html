About Google Flights

Google Flights helps you find the best fares, track price trends, and book flights from hundreds of airlines worldwide. We combine real-time pricing, AI-driven price tracking, and a single search across the world's biggest airline catalogs to make travel planning effortless.

Our mission

We believe travel should be transparent. Google Flights does not sell tickets directly - we surface the best options from airlines and online travel agencies so you can compare and choose the right itinerary at the right price.
