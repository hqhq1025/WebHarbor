About

Our organization

TED is a non-profit headquartered in New York. We operate as TED Conferences LLC, owned by The Sapling Foundation.

How TED works

TED's mission is supported by membership, sponsorship, conference revenue, and individual giving. We re-invest in talks, translations, TED-Ed, the Audio Collective, and global TEDx events.

Leadership

The TED leadership team includes the Head of TED, programming directors, the TED Fellows team, and the TED-Ed team.
