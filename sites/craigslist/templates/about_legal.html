legal

craigslist publishes statutory disclosures, takedown procedures, and a summary of recent litigation history here. this page is not legal advice.

recent statutes that affect postings

  • fair housing act — housing postings may not exclude tenants on protected-class grounds. see discrimination & fair housing.
  • FOSTA-SESTA (2018) — personals categories were removed in 2018 in response to federal sex-trafficking legislation.
  • california consumer privacy act — california residents may request a copy of stored personal data; see privacy policy.
  • section 230 — craigslist relies on common-carrier protections for user-generated postings.

takedown requests

copyright takedowns under 17 U.S.C. §512 should be sent in writing to copyright@example.test with the posting URL, the copyrighted work, and a good-faith statement.

court ordered records

law enforcement should submit subpoenas through the legal-process portal listed in our terms of use.
