privacy policy

this is a plain-english summary. the controlling document is the full policy at privacy@example.test.

what we collect

  • email and password (hashed) when you create an account
  • IP address and user agent for each posting and login
  • any phone number you attach to a posting or relay reply
  • saved searches and saved listings for the lifetime of the account
  • messages sent through the craigslist relay (retained for 30 days)

what we do not collect

  • third-party advertising identifiers
  • browsing history outside craigslist.org
  • location beyond the region you opt in to (sf bay area, etc.)

your rights

california residents may request a copy of stored personal data, request deletion, or opt out of the limited data sharing described above. send the request to privacy@example.test from the email associated with the account.
