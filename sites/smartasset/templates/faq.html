SmartAsset FAQ

Is SmartAsset really free?

Yes. Every calculator, article and the advisor matching service is free. We earn revenue from advisors who pay to receive client referrals, not from end-users.

How do you vet financial advisors?

Every advisor in the SmartAdvisor network is a registered investment adviser with a clean compliance record, operates under a fiduciary standard, and meets minimum experience and AUM thresholds. See the methodology page.

Are the calculator results accurate?

The math is deterministic and uses official IRS and FRBNY data sources. The output is a planning estimate — not legal or tax advice for your specific situation.

Do you sell my information?

No. We never sell calculator inputs. When you complete the advisor matching quiz, your contact info is shared only with the up-to-three advisors you are matched with.

Can I opt out of advisor outreach?

Yes — reply "STOP" to any text, click the unsubscribe link in any email, or contact us at contact.

What states do you cover?

All 50 states and DC. State-level calculators apply that state's tax rules. See the states index.

Do you offer financial advice directly?

No. SmartAsset is a publisher and a referral service. Personalized advice comes from the registered advisor you're matched with.

How often are articles updated?

Tax content is reviewed quarterly. Markets content is refreshed when material data changes. Each SmartReads article shows a last-updated date.

Can I delete my account?

Yes — from Account Settings or by emailing support. We delete all PII within 30 days.

What's the SmartMoney Minute?

A free weekly newsletter sent every Tuesday at 6 a.m. ET. Subscribe here.
