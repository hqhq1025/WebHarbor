Contact the BBC

Whether you have a story to tell, feedback on a programme, or want to get in touch with a specific department, the BBC has a number of ways for audiences to reach us.

Story tips and editorial enquiries

BBC News welcomes story tips, eyewitness contributions, and editorial feedback. Use the comment and account features on any article page to share corrections, send a tip, or respond to coverage. Confidential and secure contact options are available for sensitive material.

Complaints and corrections

Complaints about BBC content, conduct or services are handled by BBC Audience Services. Visit the BBC Complaints website for the standard complaints process and the right of appeal to the BBC Executive Complaints Unit and Ofcom.

BBC News press office

Journalists requesting interviews with presenters, programme images, or background on BBC News output should contact the BBC News press office. Out-of-hours media enquiries are routed to the duty press officer.
