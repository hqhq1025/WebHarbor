Help & FAQs

Common questions about using this BBC News mirror.

Creating an account

Use the Register link in the top-right to create an account. You need an email, username, and password. A demo account is also available if you just want to look around: demo@bbcnews.local / demodemo.

Reading list vs. bookmarks

  • Reading list — saving stories to read later, with optional folders and notes. Designed for longer-term organisation and exporting as digests.
  • Bookmarks — one-click save from any story page for quick access. Great for stories you want to come back to soon.

What's a digest?

A digest bundles a set of articles (from your reading list, bookmarks, or the latest news) into an exportable file — email newsletter, RSS, or plain text. Digests are useful for archiving, sharing, or sending stories to yourself for later reading.

Topic alerts

Subscribe to any BBC News section (World, UK, Tech, etc.) to receive updates when new stories publish. Choose daily, weekly, or breaking-only frequency.

Can I comment?

Yes — signed-in users can post comments on any article. You can edit or delete your own comments from the article page.
