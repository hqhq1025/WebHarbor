Privacy Policy

Effective: January 8, 2026

Information we collect

  • Account information (name, email, password hash).
  • Learning activity (course progress, quiz attempts, peer-review submissions).
  • Payment information processed by our card processor — Coursera never stores raw card numbers.
  • Device and usage signals (browser, OS, IP, anonymized telemetry).

How we use information

  • Deliver the courses, certificates, and recommendations you request.
  • Power the search experience and improve the quality of our catalog.
  • Send transactional emails (enrollment confirmations, deadlines).
  • Marketing email — you can opt out at any time from Account › Notifications.

Sharing with partners

When you enroll in a course, the partnering university or company receives the data needed to deliver the course (e.g., name, completion status). Degrees and MasterTrack programs receive more detailed admissions information that you submit.

Your rights

  • Access your data: Account › Edit profile.
  • Export your learning record: request from Help Center.
  • Delete your account: Account › Delete — irreversible.
  • EU/UK/EEA residents: contact dpo@coursera.org for GDPR data-subject requests.
  • California residents: contact privacy@coursera.org for CCPA requests.

Cookies

Coursera uses cookies for authentication, preferences, and aggregated analytics. Manage cookie preferences from Account › Privacy.

See also: Terms of Use.
