How a MasterTrack works

  1. Take the modular MasterTrack online (typically 4–7 months).
  2. Earn a university-issued certificate that you can share on LinkedIn.
  3. Apply to the full master's — your certificate work may transfer for credit.
  4. Finish on campus (or fully online for select degrees).
