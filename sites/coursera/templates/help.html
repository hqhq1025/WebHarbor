Help Center

Find answers to common questions about courses, certificates, payments, and your account.

Getting started

How do I enroll in a course?

Find a course on Coursera, open its page, click Enroll for Free or Start 7-day Free Trial, and follow the prompts. Most courses can be audited for free; you can also pay to receive a certificate.

How long do I have to finish a course?

Most courses are self-paced and you can finish at your own speed. Specializations and Professional Certificates have suggested timelines (3–6 months) but no hard deadlines.

What is the difference between a Course, a Specialization, and a Professional Certificate?

A Course covers one focused topic. A Specialization bundles several related courses into a learning path. A Professional Certificate is a job-aligned credential built by industry partners such as Google, IBM, and Meta.

Payments and refunds

What is Coursera Plus?

Coursera Plus is an annual subscription that unlocks 7,000+ courses, specializations, and professional certificates for one price. Learn more.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — most paid courses and specializations come with a 14-day money-back guarantee. Visit your Account page to request a refund.

Do you offer financial aid?

Many courses offer financial aid for learners who cannot afford the fee. Apply from the course page; approvals typically take up to 15 business days.

Certificates and credit

How do I receive my certificate?

Once you complete every required item with a passing grade, your certificate appears in your Account. You can share it directly to LinkedIn or download it as a PDF.

Are Coursera certificates accredited?

Many specializations and degrees are credit-eligible through partner universities. Look for the Credit Eligible tag on the course page.

Account and technical support

How do I change my password?

Go to Account → Settings and click Change password.

I cannot watch a video — what should I try?

Try refreshing your browser, switching to Chrome or Firefox, and disabling browser extensions. If the issue persists, contact our support team.

How do I delete my account?

From your Account page, scroll to the bottom and select Delete account.

Still need help?

Email our learner support team at support@coursera.org or browse our community forums for peer-to-peer answers.
