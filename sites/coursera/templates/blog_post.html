Across our learner community we see the same pattern: the people who finish what they start are the ones who block out 45 minutes, three times a week, and treat their learning the way they treat their best work meetings — protected, prepared, and consistent.

The good news is that you do not need to overhaul your calendar to make that happen. In the rest of this post we walk through the playbook a handful of our most successful learners use to keep momentum, hold themselves accountable, and finish strong.

Block calendar time before you enrol

Before clicking Enroll, look at the next 8 weeks of your calendar and place three 45-minute holds per week. The act of pre-committing to time is the single biggest predictor of completion in our internal data.

Use the mobile app on transit

Download two weeks of content to your phone. A 20-minute commute that used to be doom-scroll time becomes the home of your refresher lectures.

Tell one person what you are working on

A learning buddy — even one you only text once a week — increases the probability you finish by roughly 1.6× in our cohort analyses. Find your buddy before week 1 is over.
