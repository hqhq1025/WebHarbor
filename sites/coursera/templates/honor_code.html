Coursera Honor Code

Effective: March 1, 2026

By enrolling in a course, you agree to the Coursera Honor Code. This pledge is what makes our community trustworthy for employers, universities, and fellow learners.

I will:

  • Register for only one account.
  • Submit only my own work on graded assignments.
  • Not share solutions to graded assignments — including in study groups and on external sites.
  • Properly cite any external sources I draw from.
  • Use Coursera AI tutors and external generative AI only as study aids, not as graded answer producers.

I will not:

  • Share my account credentials.
  • Post copyrighted material that I did not author.
  • Engage in any form of academic dishonesty, including plagiarism, collusion, or impersonation.
  • Pay another person, or be paid, to complete coursework.

Consequences

Violations may result in any of the following:

  • A failing grade on the assignment or course
  • Revocation of certificate or completion record
  • Account suspension or permanent ban
  • Notification to the credential-issuing partner university or employer

Report a violation

If you observe academic dishonesty, please email honor-code@coursera.org or use the in-course "Report" link on any forum post.
