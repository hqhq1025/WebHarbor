Refund Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026 · View change log

Two-week refund window

For paid courses, Professional Certificates, and Specializations, you can request a full refund within 14 days of payment, as long as you have not earned the course certificate.

Subscription products (Coursera Plus, Specializations)

  • Monthly subscriptions: refundable within 7 days of the latest renewal.
  • Annual Coursera Plus: refundable within 14 days of purchase or renewal.
  • Refunds are issued in the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Degree and MasterTrack programs

Refunds for degree tuition and MasterTrack programs are governed by the partnering university's policy. Visit your program page for details, or contact your enrollment counselor.

How to request a refund

  1. Go to your Account › Purchase history.
  2. Find the order and click Request refund.
  3. Confirm the refund reason. The funds are returned within 5–10 business days.

Non-refundable items

  • Already-earned certificates
  • Coursera for Business or Campus license seats
  • Enrollments made through employer or institutional sponsorship
