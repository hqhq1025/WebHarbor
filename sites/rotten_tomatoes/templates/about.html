About Rotten Tomatoes

Rotten Tomatoes is the world's most trusted recommendation resource for quality entertainment. Launched in 1998, the site provides the Tomatometer score, audience score, and editorial coverage of every major film and TV release.

The Tomatometer

The Tomatometer score represents the percentage of professional film critic reviews that are positive for a given film or TV show. A score of 60% or higher is Fresh; below 60% is Rotten. When a film receives a Tomatometer score of at least 75% with a minimum of 80 reviews (and 5 from Top Critics), it earns the Certified Fresh badge.

Audience Score

The audience score reflects the percentage of users who have rated a movie or TV show positively. Scores of 60% or higher are Upright; below that, Spilled.
