About Cambridge Dictionary

The Cambridge Dictionary is the world's most popular online dictionary for learners of English, with definitions, translations, pronunciations, and example sentences across more than a hundred languages.

Our editorial principles

Every entry is written and reviewed by professional lexicographers based at Cambridge University Press & Assessment. Definitions are grounded in the Cambridge English Corpus — a 2-billion-word collection of real, spoken and written English.

What you'll find here

  • Dictionary: meanings, pronunciations (UK & US), examples, and CEFR levels (A1–C2).
  • Thesaurus: synonyms, antonyms, and near-meanings.
  • Grammar: over 200 topic pages covering present-day usage with example sentences.
  • Translate: instant translations powered by Microsoft.
  • Plus: quizzes, word lists, and word games for free.

Contact

Editorial enquiries should be addressed to the lexicography team at Cambridge University Press & Assessment, Cambridge, United Kingdom.
