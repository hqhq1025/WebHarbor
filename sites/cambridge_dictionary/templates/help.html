Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How do I look up a word?

Type the word into the search box at the top of any page and press Enter, or click the magnifying glass. The dictionary supports both UK and US spellings and will suggest matches as you type.

What do the CEFR level labels mean?

Every entry tagged with a level (A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2) corresponds to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages. A1 is beginner, C2 is mastery.

How do I save a word to my list?

Sign in to your free Cambridge Dictionary account, open any word page, and click Add to word list. Your saved words appear under My Account.

Where do the audio recordings come from?

Each headword has separate UK and US recordings, available at /static/audio/uk/<word>.mp3 and /static/audio/us/<word>.mp3.

How can I report a typo or missing word?

Reach out to the lexicography team via the editorial email listed on our About page. Every report is read.

Is the dictionary free?

Yes — the dictionary, thesaurus, grammar, and translation tools are completely free. Cambridge Dictionary Plus features additional quizzes and word games, also at no cost.
