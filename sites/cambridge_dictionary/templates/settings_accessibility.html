Accessibility settings

Cambridge Dictionary works hard to stay usable for every reader. Choose the options that help you read, hear, and navigate the dictionary. Preferences are stored locally on this device.

Reading font

Increases letter spacing, line height, and bottom-weighted letters to reduce flipping.

CEFR colour palette

Replaces the A1–C2 swatches with an Okabe–Ito-derived ramp distinguishable under deuteranopia and protanopia.

IPA tooltip

Adds tabindex=0 to every IPA span so screen-reader / keyboard users can review pronunciation.
