Mayo Clinic strives to make every visit as seamless as possible. The information on this page applies to all three Mayo Clinic campuses (Rochester, Jacksonville, and Phoenix) unless otherwise noted. Specific procedures may vary by location, and your care team will provide details tailored to your situation.

If you have questions, contact your care coordinator or the Patient and Visitor Information desk at your campus. For international patients, the international office at each campus offers dedicated coordinators who can assist with translation, accommodations, and travel logistics.

For appointments, please use the Request an Appointment form. For physician referrals, see Refer a Patient.
