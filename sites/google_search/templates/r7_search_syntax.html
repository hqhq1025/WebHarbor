Filter chains

Operators stack — site:nytimes.com intitle:climate after:2024-01-01 returns only NYTimes pages with "climate" in the title published after Jan 1 2024.

  • Stack site: + intitle: + after: for tight news filtering
  • Use -site: to exclude a single domain from broad results
  • Wrap multi-word operands in quotes — intitle:"sea level rise"
  • filetype:pdf is the fastest way to find primary documents
