Google can't hear you. Voice search requires permission to use your microphone, but the browser denied or revoked access.

How to fix this

  1. Click the lock icon in your browser's address bar.
  2. Open the Site permissions menu.
  3. Set Microphone to Allow.
  4. Reload this page and click the microphone icon again.
