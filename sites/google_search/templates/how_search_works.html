How Search Works

Every time you search, thousands of factors help Google surface the most relevant and useful results — in a fraction of a second.

1. Crawling & indexing

Google discovers pages on the web by following links, sitemaps, and submissions.

2. Ranking signals

Hundreds of ranking signals — from page relevance to expertise to site quality — determine which pages are most useful for a query.

3. Serving results

We present results in formats best suited to your query: text results, images, videos, news, maps, and knowledge panels.
