Contact Us

We welcome questions, feedback, and corrections from our readers. Please review the categories below to make sure your message reaches the right team.

General questions and feedback

For general questions about the Drugs.com website, suggestions for improvement, or comments on our content, email feedback@drugs.com. We read every message, although we cannot always respond individually.

Medical questions

Drugs.com staff cannot provide individualized medical advice. If you have a question about your medication, dose, side effects, or interactions, please contact your physician, pharmacist, or local poison control center. In the U.S., you can also reach the Poison Help line 24 hours a day at 1-800-222-1222.

Editorial corrections

If you've found an error in one of our drug monographs, news articles, or other editorial content, email editor@drugs.com with the page URL and a description of the issue. Our medical editors review every submission and update content when needed.

Press and media

Members of the press requesting interviews, statements, or background information should contact press@drugs.com. Please include your name, outlet, deadline, and a brief description of your story.

Business and advertising

For partnership, licensing, or advertising inquiries, contact business@drugs.com. We do not accept unsolicited content submissions.
