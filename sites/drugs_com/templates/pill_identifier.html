How to use the Pill Identifier

  1. Enter the imprint code on your pill
  2. Select the shape (optional)
  3. Select the color (optional)
  4. Click Search

Note: The imprint is the number, letters, or markings on the pill.

Example: 'M367' for a white oval pill.

Safety warning: If you cannot identify a pill, contact Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222.
