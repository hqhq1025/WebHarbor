Pregnancy Drug Safety

Find information about medication safety during pregnancy. Listed drugs include their FDA pregnancy risk category and specific guidance for use during pregnancy and breastfeeding.

Important: Always consult your healthcare provider before taking any medication during pregnancy or while breastfeeding. No medication should be considered completely safe during pregnancy.

FDA Pregnancy Categories

CategoryMeaning
AAdequate studies show no risk to the fetus in the first trimester, and no risk in later trimesters.
BAnimal studies show no risk, but no adequate human studies; or animal studies show adverse effects but human studies show no risk.
CAnimal studies show adverse effects and there are no adequate human studies, or no animal or human studies have been done.
DEvidence of human fetal risk exists, but benefits may outweigh risks in certain situations.
XStudies show fetal abnormalities. Risks outweigh benefits. Contraindicated in pregnancy.
NNot classified by the FDA.
