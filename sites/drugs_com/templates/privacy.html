Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, post a drug review, save a medication to My Med List, or contact customer support. This may include your name, email address, username, and any health-related information you choose to submit. We also automatically collect certain information about your device, browser, and how you interact with the Service, including IP address, pages visited, and search terms used.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the Service; to personalize your experience; to communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and newsletters you've subscribed to; and to comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Cookies and tracking

Drugs.com uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and analyze how the Service is used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the Service may not function properly without them.

Health information

Drugs.com is not a covered entity under HIPAA. However, we treat health information you submit with care and apply industry-standard safeguards. Drug reviews are publicly visible by design; do not include information you wish to keep private.

Sharing your information

We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Service (hosting, analytics, email delivery) under contractual confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Drugs.com, our users, or the public.

Your choices

You can review, update, or delete account information from your account settings. You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by following the unsubscribe link in any message we send.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Please contact us.
