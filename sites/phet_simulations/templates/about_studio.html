PhET Studio

PhET Studio is the in-browser authoring environment the project uses to prototype, configure, and adapt our HTML5 simulations. Studio lets simulation designers tweak parameters, accessibility wiring, and translation strings without rebuilding the underlying source.

Custom simulations

Teachers, district teams, and research partners can request a custom PhET simulation tailored to a specific curriculum or assessment. Custom builds reuse the same accessibility, internationalization, and research-based interaction patterns as the published library.

Developer tooling

The PhET libraries are open source under a Creative Commons license. The development documentation covers the simulation architecture, the scenery graphics layer, the axon reactive model layer, and the simulation lifecycle from prototype through QA. Build a Custom Sim using the same components our team uses every day.

Get involved

If you would like to collaborate on a Studio build or contribute to the PhET codebase, write to us via the contact form.
