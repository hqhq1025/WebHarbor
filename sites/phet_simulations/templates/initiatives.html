PhET Initiatives

PhET Initiatives extend our free interactive simulations with focused programs that broaden access, equity, and research impact across STEM learning.

Accessibility

The Inclusive Design initiative embeds keyboard navigation, screen reader support, sound effects, and alternative input across every HTML5 simulation. See the Accessibility page for the current feature matrix and ongoing studies.

Global PhET

Global PhET coordinates translation, regional partnerships, and offline distribution so PhET simulations reach classrooms in low- bandwidth and remote environments. Browse the translated simulations catalog for the {{ stats.languages }} supported languages.

Data Fluency

Data Fluency simulations and lesson plans help students reason about uncertainty, evidence, distributions, and modeling — across math, science, and social-studies contexts.

DEIB in STEM Education

Our diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging program partners with educators and researchers to embed culturally responsive practice in simulation-based STEM learning, and to broaden participation in science and mathematics.

Research collaborations

If you lead an education-research project that would benefit from PhET partnership, write to us through the contact form.
