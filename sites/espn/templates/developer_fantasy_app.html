Build a Fantasy App with ESPN

Integration guide for the ESPN mirror's fantasy-app surface. Covers polling cadences, the GraphQL-min v3 query patterns, and the webhook contract used by the live-scores ticker.

Quickstart

  1. Fetch a team's basics via /api/v3-graphql with {"query":"team(slug)"}.
  2. Subscribe (mirror-style) to score events by polling /api/events?limit=50 every 60 seconds.
  3. For test-driven flows, POST mock payloads to /webhook/score-update; the ack id is deterministic.
  4. Send your own telemetry to /api/telemetry; nothing is persisted, but the response is deterministic and signed.
