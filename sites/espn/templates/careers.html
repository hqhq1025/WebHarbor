Careers at ESPN

Help build the future of sports media. ESPN is hiring across production, technology, business, and editorial.

Why ESPN

  • Mission — Serve sports fans anytime, anywhere.
  • Scale — 8 U.S. networks, ESPN+, mobile, podcasts, and live events.
  • Disney benefits — Comprehensive health coverage, 401(k) match, paid parental leave, park access, employee discounts.
  • Hybrid & remote — Many roles are hybrid Bristol, hybrid Los Angeles, or fully remote within the U.S.

Internships & Early Career

The ESPN Internship Program is a 12-week paid program with summer, fall, and spring tracks. Disney's Professional Internships span Production, Engineering, Editorial, and Business Operations. Applications open quarterly.

How to Apply

All ESPN openings are posted on the official Disney Careers site. Search "ESPN" on jobs.disneycareers.com, create an account, and submit your resume. ESPN does not accept unsolicited resumes by email.
