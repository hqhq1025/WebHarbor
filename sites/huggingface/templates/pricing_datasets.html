FAQ

Are public datasets truly free?

Yes — public datasets carry no storage or bandwidth charge. Only private datasets count against quota.

What's the largest single file I can upload?

100 GB per file via LFS. Use huggingface-cli upload-large-folder for sharded uploads.

Can I bring my own S3 bucket?

Enterprise plans support bring-your-own-bucket (BYOB) — contact sales.
