FAQ

Is there a free tier?

Yes — every Space starts on CPU Basic at no cost. Upgrade only when you need a GPU.

How is billing calculated?

Per-second metering. A paused Space accrues storage cost only ($0.001/GB/hr).

Can I auto-sleep idle Spaces?

Set sleep_time in your Space metadata. Default is 24h on Pro, 48h on Enterprise.
