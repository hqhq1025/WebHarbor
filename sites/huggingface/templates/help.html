Help Center

Find answers to common questions about the Hub, or contact support.

Getting started

  • How do I create an account?
    Click "Sign Up" in the top right and fill in your email, username, and a password.
  • How do I upload a model?
    Use the huggingface_hub Python library, or drag and drop files in the web UI.
  • Is it free?
    Public models, datasets, and spaces are free forever. PRO and Enterprise plans add more features.

Billing

  • How is inference billed?
    By the hour of hardware used. See the pricing page for details.
  • Can I pause an endpoint?
    Yes — go to your endpoint's page and click "Pause". You stop being billed while paused.

Contact us

Can't find what you need? Email support@hf.co or open a discussion in the community.
